Xico Games operates with a commitment to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Offline Use

Xico Games allows the use of some features in its games offline (without an internet connection). Offline play will preclude the collection of any information about You and Your gameplay by Xico Games or third parties.

Online Use

Xico Games does not collect personally identifiable information from users. However, third-party services integrated into our games may collect and store certain information on their servers.

Non-Personal Information

Xico Games allows third parties to collect non-personal, non-identifiable information ("Non-Personal Information"). We do not know the identity of the users from whom this information is collected.

Non-Personal Information collected by third parties may include:

  • Type of operating system (e.g., Android, iOS)
  • Type of browser (e.g., Chrome, Safari)
  • Screen resolution
  • Language settings
  • Device Vendor (e.g., Samsung, Apple)
  • General geo-location (no street or city-level data)

This information is collected strictly for service functionality and performance analysis.

Persistent User Identifier

Third parties may collect a persistent user identifier, such as an Advertising ID (IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android). This identifier is used strictly for internal operations, including serving contextual ads.

Xico Games does not allow third parties to collect personally identifiable information or link personal data to a persistent identifier.

Ad Serving Technology

Xico Games may include proprietary or third-party ad-serving technologies that use cookies, tracking pixels, or other methods to collect data related to advertisements. This includes:

  • Device IDs
  • Information about your hardware and browser
  • Advertisements served and user interactions
  • In-game location of ads
  • Duration of ad views

Ad-serving technologies are integrated into our games. If you do not wish to participate, do not play our games.

Cookies

This Service does not explicitly use cookies. However, third-party services used in the app may employ cookies to collect information and improve their services. You can accept or refuse cookies and be notified when a cookie is sent to your device. If you disable cookies, some portions of the Service may not function properly.

Analytics and Data Tracking

Xico Games uses analytics tools such as Google Firebase Analytics and Unity Analytics to understand how players interact with our games. These tools help us gather insights about user behavior and improve gameplay experiences. Firebase Analytics and Unity Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect gameplay data, including session duration, device type, and interactions within the game.

The information collected is transmitted to and stored by Google and Unity Technologies. It is used to evaluate gameplay, generate reports, and provide statistical analysis that helps us enhance user experience.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee unauthorized access will never occur.

In-App Purchases (IAP)

Some features or items in our games may be available for purchase through app stores (Google, Apple, etc.). All in-app purchases (IAP) are made with your full knowledge and consent. Xico Games is not responsible for refunds or claims regarding IAPs. This policy applies only when in-app purchases are available in our games.

User Rights

If you are a resident of certain regions (e.g., the European Union under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements

To exercise these rights, contact us at xicogames786@gmail.com.

Information Sharing

We may share information in the following situations:

  • To comply with legal requirements, such as subpoenas or governmental requests.
  • To enforce our Privacy Policy or Terms of Use, including investigating violations.
  • To detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • To respond to claims that content in our games violates third-party rights.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, users, or the public.
  • In cases of business mergers, acquisitions, or changes in company control.
  • With your approval, to provide services requested by you.
  • To enable our partners and affiliates to deliver contextual advertisements.
