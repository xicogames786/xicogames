Overview

Xico Games ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile game applications available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our games.

By downloading or using any of our applications, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please do not download or use our games.

Plain-English Summary: We collect limited technical and gameplay data to improve our games and show relevant ads. We do not sell your personal information. We use Google AdMob for advertising and Firebase Analytics to understand how our games are played.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information through our games and the third-party services we use:

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID, Apple IDFA/IDFV), device language, and time zone.
  • Usage Data: Session duration, levels played, in-game events, features accessed, crash reports, and performance diagnostics.
  • Network Information: IP address (used to determine approximate geographic region for content localization), mobile carrier, and connection type (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Ad Interaction Data: Advertisements displayed to you, ad impressions, and whether you clicked on an advertisement.

Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Contact Inquiries: If you contact us via email or our website contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.
  • Support Requests: Information you include in a support request or bug report.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account registration in our games.
  • We do not collect your full name, postal address, phone number, or government-issued identification.
  • We do not collect payment information (all in-app purchases are handled directly by Apple and Google).
  • We do not collect the contents of your messages or contacts.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Operate and Improve Games: To run the game, save progress (where applicable), fix bugs, and improve gameplay features.
  • Analytics: To understand how players interact with our games so we can make better design decisions and balance difficulty.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob, including personalized ads where you have consented, or non-personalized ads where consent has not been given.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and other harmful or unauthorized activity.

Third-Party Services

Our games integrate third-party services that may collect information independently under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their policies directly.

The following third-party services may be included in our applications:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Firebase Analytics (app analytics)
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)
  • Google Play Services (on Android)
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (on iOS)

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements within our games. AdMob may use the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • Your approximate location (country/region level) derived from your IP address
  • Your device type and operating system version
  • In-app behavior and interaction patterns

AdMob may serve personalized advertisements based on your past browsing and app behavior, or non-personalized advertisements that are contextually matched to the content.

Your Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID, or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."
  • iOS (14+): When prompted, choose "Ask App Not to Track." Alternatively, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for our app.
  • Google Ad Settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad preferences.

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use its services, please visit Google's Partner Sites Policy. To view Google's Privacy Policy, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect and analyze usage data from our games. Firebase Analytics helps us understand which features are most used, how long players engage with our games, where players encounter difficulty, and how our games perform technically.

Firebase Analytics collects data such as:

  • App usage events and in-game actions
  • Session length and frequency
  • Device and operating system information
  • App version and installation source
  • Approximate geographic region (country/region level)

Firebase Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized for analytical purposes. We do not use Firebase Analytics to personally identify individual players.

You can learn more about Firebase's data collection practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Disabling Analytics Collection

You can disable Firebase Analytics data collection by resetting your Advertising ID on Android or enabling Limit Ad Tracking on iOS. Some of our games may also offer an in-game privacy settings option.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers (such as Google) who assist us in operating our games and services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, court order, or government authority.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Xico Games, our players, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, in which case your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months as governed by Firebase Analytics default settings.
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days.
  • Support correspondence is retained for up to 2 years.
  • Advertising identifiers are managed by Google AdMob according to Google's data retention policies.

When data is no longer needed, we delete it securely or anonymize it so that it can no longer be associated with any individual.

Children's Privacy

Some of our games may be suitable for general audiences, including children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with applicable laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union, and the UK Children's Code.

Games rated for children (under 13): For any game designated as directed at children under 13, we disable personalized advertising and limit data collection to only what is necessary to operate the game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 for marketing or profiling purposes.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@xicogames.com so we can delete the information.

Our general-audience games that are not specifically directed at children use Google AdMob with child-directed treatment flags set appropriately based on applicable store ratings.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK

Under the GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Data portability where technically feasible
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

For California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Delete personal information we hold about you
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@xicogames.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we update the policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

For material changes, we will provide notice through our games or by posting a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of our games after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@xicogames.com
  • General Contact: contact@xicogames.com
  • Website: Contact Form
